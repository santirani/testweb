The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi at the inauguration of the curtain raiser activities of the ‘Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav’ India@75, in Ahmedabad, Gujarat on March 12, 2021. The Governor of Gujarat, Shri Acharya Devvrat, the Chief Minister of Gujarat, Shri Vijay Rupani, the Minister of State for Culture and Tourism (Independent Charge), Shri Prahlad Singh Patel and other dignitaries are also seen.
